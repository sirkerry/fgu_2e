# 2E - Exploding Damage

FGU extension for the official 2E (AD&D 2nd Edition) ruleset.

Classic exploding-dice house rule for all damage rolls — weapons and spells alike. When a damage die rolls its maximum possible value, it rerolls and adds the new result to the total, repeating for as long as it keeps exploding.

## How to Use

Just enable the extension and load a 2E campaign — no options, no setup. Every weapon and spell damage roll automatically uses exploding dice:

- Roll damage as normal (melee, ranged, or spell).
- Any die that lands on its maximum value (a 6 on a d6, a 4 on a d4, etc.) automatically rerolls and adds to the total, chaining as long as it keeps exploding.
- Rolls that don't hit the maximum are completely unaffected — no extra dice, no message changes.

## Known Limitation

On a critical hit, 2E's dice-doubling rule adds a fresh set of dice for the crit bonus — the original damage dice still explode normally, but the extra doubled dice from the critical hit do not themselves explode.

## Compatibility

- Official 2E (AD&D 2nd Edition) ruleset
